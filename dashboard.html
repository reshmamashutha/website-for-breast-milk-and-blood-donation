BREAST-MILK DONATION PROCESS

The donation process from the time you arrive at center until the time you leave

REGISTRATION

You need to complete a very simple registration form which contatins all required contact information to enter in the donation process. You can also finish this process online using our website.

SCREENING

A drop of blood from your finger will be taken for a simple test to ensure that your blood levels are proper enough for donation. And you will be screend for any blood related diseases.

DONATION

After passing screening test successfully you will be directed to a donor room for donation. You will be given a pump to express the milk.

REFRESHMENT

You can also stay in the sitting room until you feel strong enough to leave the center. You will receive refreshments in the donation zone
